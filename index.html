How It Works

Three steps
from pixels to bricks.

STEP 01
📁

Export Your Build

Use WorldEdit or Amulet to export your Minecraft structure as a .schematic or .schem file. Or just upload a screenshot — we handle both.

STEP 02
⚡

AI Maps Every Block

Our engine reads your 3D block data and maps 200+ Minecraft block types to the nearest LEGO color and shape — accounting for stairs, slabs, glass, and more.

STEP 03
🧱

Order & Build

Get a complete BrickLink shopping cart with every part you need, a 3D preview in your browser, and step-by-step build instructions.
